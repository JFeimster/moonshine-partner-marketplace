# Engineering as Marketing — Best Plays for Moonshine Capital

## Thesis

The best lead-gen assets are not more generic articles.
They are **interactive tools** that attract, segment, qualify, and route people.

## Best Tool Ideas

### 1. Funding Match Quiz
Inputs:
- what do you need funding for?
- revenue
- time in business
- credit range
- business type
- urgency

Output:
- best-fit funding path through Moonshine Capital
- next-step CTA

### 2. Revenue-to-Funding Estimator
Inputs:
- monthly revenue
- deposits
- business type
- urgency

Output:
- estimated capital band
- likely funding path
- docs checklist

### 3. Startup Funding Planner
Inputs:
- credit score range
- income
- entity status
- funding goal

Output:
- likely startup path
- readiness issues
- next steps

### 4. Equipment Finance Calculator
Inputs:
- equipment cost
- down payment
- years in business
- credit band

Output:
- finance path
- rough payment scenario
- CTA

### 5. DSCR / Rental Deal Precheck
Inputs:
- purchase price
- rent
- rehab
- projected cash flow

Output:
- likely fit
- readiness warning
- next-step CTA

### 6. Marketplace Cashflow Tool
Inputs:
- platform
- sales
- payout lag
- inventory cycle

Output:
- cashflow stress estimate
- capital options
- CTA

## Distribution Strategy

Each tool should exist in 3 versions:
1. standalone page
2. embeddable widget
3. gated lead-magnet version

## Key Principle

A tool should do 4 jobs:
1. attract traffic
2. segment intent
3. qualify the lead
4. trigger a funnel

If it only looks cool, it is marketing jewelry.
