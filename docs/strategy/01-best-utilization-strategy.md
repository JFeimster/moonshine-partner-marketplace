# Best Utilization of the Funding Catalog for Affiliate Partners

## The Real Play

The best use of this catalog is **not** “give partners a giant list of funding products.”
That leaks value and invites bypass.

The smarter move is:

> Turn the catalog into a **controlled lead-generation and routing system** where partners distribute front-end assets, while Moonshine Capital owns the qualification logic, backend mapping, tracking, and fulfillment path.

## Best Utilization Models

### 1. Product-Specific Lead Pages
Each partner shares a single-offer category page:
- startup funding
- business line of credit
- fast working capital
- equipment financing
- real estate bridge capital
- e-commerce capital

**Why it works**
- clearer CTA
- easier messaging
- less user confusion
- cleaner attribution

### 2. Vertical Lead Funnels
Examples:
- funding for contractors
- funding for trucking companies
- capital for Amazon sellers
- startup funding for founders
- real estate investor capital
- self-employed funding

**Why it works**
- better message-market fit
- better SEO
- better paid traffic performance

### 3. Problem-Solution Pages
Examples:
- need money for payroll
- got denied by the bank
- need fast inventory funding
- need startup capital with no revenue
- need equipment now

This usually converts harder than product-first language.

### 4. Embedded Widgets
Examples:
- funding fit quiz
- startup funding checker
- revenue-to-funding estimator
- equipment financing calculator

Partners place these on their own sites, but Moonshine Capital owns the routing and capture.

### 5. Partner Microsites
For stronger affiliates:
- partner-specific landing hub
- tracked application flow
- niche-specific content
- attribution baked in

### 6. Gated Lead Magnets
Examples:
- funding readiness checklist
- startup capital blueprint
- founder financing guide
- contractor capital cheat sheet

## Strongest Recommendation

Use a 3-layer model:

### Layer 1: Public acquisition
- niche pages
- quizzes
- tools
- widgets
- blog content
- local pages

### Layer 2: Conversion layer
- prequal form
- funding-match quiz
- tracked application flow

### Layer 3: Backend orchestration
- CRM capture
- partner attribution
- hidden product map
- internal routing
- follow-up automation

That is the business.
Anything else is just affiliate theater.
